CREATE

Masking Tape

A high-grade & water-resistant crepe paper Masking Tape offers pressure-sensitive quality. It is generally used for drawing on the wall, painting on paper, and other professional and personal purposes.

CREATE

Double Sided Tissue Tape

Made with tissue paper as base material and coating on both side with pressure sensitive adhesive, then make it in rolls with releasing paper. Excellent performance of temperature. Special formulated adhesive provides strong holding power.

CREATE

Duct Tape

Duct tape with a natural rubber adhesive and water resistant polyethylene backing. It provides easy tear and good conform ability. The adhesive has excellent tack and is a great choice for sticking to Metal, Glass, Plastic, Sealed concrete and more.
