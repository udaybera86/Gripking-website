PE Foam Tape

Applications

Double Side Acrylic Tape

Advantages

Clear foamed acrylic tape 6110 can adhere to most metals, even steel (holding up to 7kg per 25mm of tape).

When used correctly, the bond strength is strong enough to rival welds, screws, rivets and other mechanical fixing.

Applications

The closed-cell foam construction makes the tape ideal for both internal and external fixings in all weather conditions.
